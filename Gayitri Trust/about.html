About the Trust

Gayatri Trust is established to nurture Vedic dharma and educational excellence among Brahmin families connected with GSSS School, Siddhartha Nagar. The trust conducts religious activities, samskaras, and educational initiatives in alignment with its registered deed.

The trust works with parents, teachers, and well-wishers to support children in both spiritual and academic growth.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To cultivate a generation rooted in Sanatana dharma, Vedic values, and strong academic foundations, guided by Gayatri upasana.

Mission

  • Encourage daily Gayatri Japa and Sandhya among students.
  • Conduct shloka, bhajan, and Pravachana sessions regularly.
  • Support the educational journey of deserving Brahmin students.

Key Activities

Poojas & Utsavas

Special Gayatri homas, Upakarma, and festival celebrations in coordination with the school and community.

Student Programs

Value education sessions, cultural competitions, and guidance for academic excellence.

Community Seva

Annadana, support to needy families, and other dharmic initiatives organized by the trust from time to time.
